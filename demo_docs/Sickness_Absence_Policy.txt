# Sickness Absence Policy — [Demo Doc 2]
==============================================

## 1. Reporting Sickness
If you are unable to work due to illness, you must:
- Notify your manager at least 1 hour before your start time.
- State the reason for your absence.
- Estimate how long you will be away.

## 2. Statutory Sick Pay (SSP)
You are entitled to SSP if you are off work for at least 4 days in a row (including weekends). 
- SSP is £116.75 per week for up to 28 weeks.
- The first 3 days of sickness are "waiting days" and are unpaid.
- You must earn at least £123 per week on average to qualify.

## 3. Fit Notes (Doctors' Note)
You can self-certify for the first 7 days of illness. 
- For any absence over 7 days, you must provide a "Fit Note" from your doctor.

[Source: ACAS Sickness Absence Guidance — Jan 2025]
