# Maternity & Paternity Policy — [Demo Doc 1]
==============================================

## 1. Statutory Maternity Leave (SML)
All employees are entitled to 52 weeks of Statutory Maternity Leave (SML) from their first day of employment. SML is made up of:
- Ordinary Maternity Leave (the first 26 weeks)
- Additional Maternity Leave (the last 26 weeks)

## 2. Statutory Maternity Pay (SMP)
If you have worked for the company for at least 26 weeks up to the 'qualifying week', you may be eligible for SMP. 
- SMP for up to 39 weeks.
- The first 6 weeks: 90% of your average weekly earnings (AWE) before tax.
- The remaining 33 weeks: £184.03 per week or 90% of your AWE (whichever is lower).

## 3. Notice Periods
You must notify HR at least 15 weeks before the expected week of childbirth. 
- You must provide the MATB1 form from your midwife.
- You must state when you want your leave to start.

[Source: UK Statutory Minimum Standards — Oct 2024]
