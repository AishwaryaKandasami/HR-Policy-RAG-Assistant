# Annual Leave & Holiday Policy — [Demo Doc 3]
==============================================

## 1. Statutory Entitlement
Full-time employees are entitled to 5.6 weeks (28 days) of paid holiday per year.
- This includes bank holidays.
- Part-time employees have a pro-rata entitlement.

## 2. Holiday Pay Calculations
During your holiday, you should receive the same pay as if you were working.
- This includes regular overtime, commission, and bonuses.
- Pay is calculated on the average weekly earnings over the previous 52 weeks.

## 3. Booking Leave
- You must give at least twice as much notice as the length of the holiday.
- Example: 2 weeks notice for a 1-week holiday.
- Your manager can refuse a holiday request as long as they provide notice.

[Source: UK Working Time Regulations — Oct 2024]
